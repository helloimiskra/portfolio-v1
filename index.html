About Me


Hello, I’m Iskra. I’m a full-stack software engineer who enjoys building applications with Ruby-on-Rails, JavaScript, React/Redux, SQL, Sinatra, and HTML/CSS.


As someone who gets fired up about technology, learning languages, and solving problems, I came to coding as a way to build solutions and to help connect people globally after working abroad for 5 years in South Korea.


A jill-of-all-trades, I am adaptable, always learning, and constantly making connections to bridge the big picture with the details. I hope to use my cross-cultural competency and insight to help companies build products and bridge the gap between their services and their international clients' needs.
